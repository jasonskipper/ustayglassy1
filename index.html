About Me

Since 2014, I have translated the flow state of flameworking borosilicate glass into form and function one can experience. My brand, Ustayglassy, has become a reminder to celebrate creation and imagination, hopefully inspiring others to do the same. I believe the genius of design can change the world. This journey has offered me growth and purpose. During my travels around US and Canada, I have collaborated and learned with others, united through passion for creation. The potential of this medium paired with a creative mind is limitless. Thanks for looking, my work would be unfinished without the interaction of others. Enjoy!
